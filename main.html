ROBERT LEHT || DRUMS

My name is Robert Leht.

Music has always played a huge part in my life - at the age of four I got my very own drumset, my favourite game as a kid was Guitar Hero and as a nine year old boy, I made my first Youtube video. After that I practiced and after nine years of polishing my skills, I started to make YT content again. My dream is to become a professional drummer one day and I'm doing everything I can right now to make that dream come true.
